iPhone 6S Screen Replacement

Step 1: Remove Bottom Screws

Use the pentalobe screwdriver to remove the bottom two screws.

Step 2: Loosen Adhesive

Use a heat source to soften the adhesive around the screen edges.

Step 3: Lift Screen

Use a suction cup and pry tool to carefully lift the screen.

Step 4: Remove Metal Plate

Unscrew the metal plate near the top and disconnect 3 ribbon cables under it.

Step 5: Replace Screen

Attach the new screen and repeat steps backwards.
