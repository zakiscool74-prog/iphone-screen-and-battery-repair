iPhone 5C Battery Replacement

Step 1: Remove Screws

Use the pentalobe screwdriver to remove the bottom two screws.

Step 2: Loosen Adhesive

Use a heat source to loosen the adhesive.

Step 3: Lift Phone

Use a suction cup to carefully pry open the phone.

Step 4: Remove Metal Plate

Unscrew the metal plate near middle right and disconnect 1 ribbon cable.

Step 5: Finding the Adhesive

Run the tip of a spudger between the battery and the headphone jack to unfold the battery adhesive tab.

Step 6: Remove the Adhesive

Pull the battery adhesive tab away from the phone. Cut the black battery adhesive tab between the two white adhesive strips, separating them. Slowly pull one of the battery adhesive strips away from the battery, toward the bottom of the iPhone. Repeat to remove the second strip.

Step 7: Remove Battery

If done correctly, you can successfully pull out the battery. Apply new adhesive and place the new battery. Repeat steps backwards.
