How to Fix an iPhone 6s Battery

Get the pentalope screw driver and screw out the bottom two screws.

Use a heat source to loosen the adhesive.

Use a suction cup to carefully pry open the phone.

Unscrew the metal plate near middle right.

There should be 1 ribbon cable under it.

Take it out using a tool.

There should be a white adhesive strip sticking out.

Use tweezers to gently pull it out downward.

Pry out the battery.

Add the adhesive from the new battery and place it in.

Repeat everything backwards.
