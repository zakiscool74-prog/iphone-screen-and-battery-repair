How to Fix an iPhone 6s Screen

Get the pentalope screw driver and screw out the bottom two screws.

Use a heat source to loosen the adhesive.

Use a suction cup to carefully pry open the phone.

Unscrew the metal plate near the top of the phone.

There should be 3 ribbon cables under it.

Take them out using a tool.

Now you have the screen and the phone base.

Buy a new screen and repeat the steps backwards.
